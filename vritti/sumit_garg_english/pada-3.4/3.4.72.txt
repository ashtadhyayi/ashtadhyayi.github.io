The affix क्त is used in denoting the agent as well as the act and the object after verbs implying motion after intransitive roots and after the verbs 1. श्लिष् 'to embrace' 2. शि 'to lie down' 3. स्था 'to stand' 4. आस् 'to sit' 5. वस् 'to dwell' 6. जन् 'to produce' 7. रुह् 'to mount' 8. जृ 'to grow old'.
