After a word ending with the गोत्र affix ण्य -- कुर्वादिभ्यो ण्यः 4.1.151 and after a गोत्र word denoting the descendant of a क्षत्रिय or a ऋषि 'seer' and after a word ending with a गोत्र affix having an indicatory ञ् , the afffix ञ् , the afffix अण् and इञ् employed in the forming युवन् -- जीवति तु वंश्ये युवा 4.1.163, descendants, are elided by the substitution of लुक्।
