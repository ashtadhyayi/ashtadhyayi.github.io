index: 3.3.140
sutra: भूते च
vritti: satishabodha
---


वृत्तिः -- (पूर्वसूत्रं संपूर्णमनुवर्तते।) हेतुहेतुमद्भावादि लिङिनमित्तं तत्र भूतार्थे लृङ् स्यात् क्रियाया अनिष्पत्तौ गम्यमानायाम् । (The अनुवृत्तिः -- of the entire prior सूत्रम् 3.3.139 लिङ्‌निमित्ते लृङ् क्रियाऽतिपत्तौ comes down into this सूत्रम्।) The affix लृँङ् is employed after a verbal root to also denote a past action, provided the conditions – such as those stated in 3.3.156 हेतुहेतुमतोर्लिङ् – for using the affix लिँङ् are satisfied and non-completion of the action is to be expressed.

Note: The affix लृँङ् has to be used in both the antecedent and the consequent clauses.


उदाहरणम् – सुवृष्टिश्चेदभविष्यत्तदा सुभिक्षमभविष्यत्। If there had been good rain (which clearly there wasn’t) then there would have been plenty of food.
