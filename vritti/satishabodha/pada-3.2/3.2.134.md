---
index: 3.2.134
sutra: आक्वेस्तच्छीलतद्धर्मतत्साधुकारिषु
vritti: satishabodha
---





वृत्तिः -- क्विपमभिव्याप्य वक्ष्यमाणाः प्रत्ययास्तच्छीलादिषु कर्तृषु बोध्याः । The affixes prescribed from here up to and including the affix ‘क्विँप्’ (that is up to 3.2.178) are to be understood to denote an agent who performs an action because of his nature/habit or sense of duty or skill.
