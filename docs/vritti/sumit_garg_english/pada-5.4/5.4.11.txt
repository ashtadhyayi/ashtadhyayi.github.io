To the affixes तर and तम (घ -- तरप्तमपौ घः 1.1.22) ordained to come after the word किम् -- अतिशायने तमबिष्ठनौ 5.3.55, or after a word ending in ए -- घकालतनेषु कालनाम्नः 6.3.17, or after a finite verb -- तिङश्च 5.3.56, द्विवचनविभज्योपपदे तरबीयसुनौ 5.3.57, or after an indeclinable, is added the affix आम् , but not if the excess belongs to a substance (and not to an action or quality)
