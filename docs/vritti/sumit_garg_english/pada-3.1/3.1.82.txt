And there is the affix श्ना after स्तम्भ , स्तुम्भ , स्कम्भ or स्कुम्भ 'to hinder', to be dull or insensible' 'to support' and स्कु 'to go by leaps', as well as the affix श्नु , when a सार्वधातुक affix follows, in denoting the agent. (Note: The first 4 धातु are not found in the धातुपाठ and are found in the sūtras, and hence called सौत्र धातु ।)
